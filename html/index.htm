Art Metamorphosis

Art, the product of creative human activity in which materials are shaped or selected to convey an idea, emotion, or visually interesting form. The word art can refer to the visual arts, including painting, sculpture, architecture, photography, decorative arts, crafts, and other visual works that combine materials or forms. We also use the word art in a more general sense to encompass other forms of creative activity, such as dance, drama, and music, or even to describe skill in almost any activity, such as “the art of bread making” or “the art of travel.”

When people ask, “What is art?” or state that something “is not art,” they usually are not seeking a philosophical definition but are instead expressing an opinion that a painting is not realistic enough, that it is offensive, or that it does not use traditional materials. Defining art too narrowly, or in a way that only affirms what we already believe, deprives us of many delightful and thought-provoking experiences. An awareness of all the things that art can be should encourage us to enjoy many different types of art, or at least to wonder why we value one type above another.

The Visual Artists' Guild (VAG)

Tagum City National High School, are composed of different organizations. One this is the Visual Artists' Guild. It is where young aspiring artists are acquiant together sharing their different skills related to arts.

All artists have freedom to draw what they want to. They are free to chose on what medium they want to use. As long as, they finished the Workshops that was organized by the officers.

The Visual Artists' Guild showcased the exemplary talents of the student's products and perfromances across the programs and workshops. It was lead by 13 different Presidents starting from the past 16 years. It was under the advisory of Mr. Oscar L. Edig.

Activities involvements are the Art Exhibits, inside & outside of the campus; the Fund Raising activity, like Hand and Face Painting & Photo Booth; Mural Paintings; Art Competioins; like Poster Making Contests, Mural Painting Contests & Art Painting Contests.

It has a Summer Art Workshop during the month of April to May of the year. Also Weekend Art Workshops in school to be led by the present officers of the guild. Some of the members are the students in the MAPEH - Enhancement Program in the Arts IV or also known as the SPA - Visual Arts.
